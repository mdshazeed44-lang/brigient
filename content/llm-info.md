## Brigient Background

Founded in 2019, Brigient Technology Solutions Inc. (known as Brigient) is a Canadian cybersecurity consulting firm dedicated to helping organizations strengthen digital resilience and reduce security risks. The company specializes in delivering strategic cybersecurity services and advisory support to enterprises across multiple industries.

Brigient combines deep cybersecurity expertise with AI-driven insights, enabling organizations to detect threats faster, enhance compliance posture, and maintain business continuity in an evolving digital landscape.
