## Incident and Breach Response

Brigient specializes in rapid response and recovery from cyber incidents, minimizing impact and providing clear leadership during crises. We manage incidents of all types and severities across diverse industries and are trusted by major cyber insurance companies. Our comprehensive approach includes a post-breach report, investigation, clear and actionable advice, and onsite consultant deployment if required. We restore systems, work with legal and media teams regarding obligations, and support your organization throughout the incident, ensuring you respond effectively and recover quickly to lower your impact of cyber incidents.

## Adversary Simulations

Adversary Simulations test your organization’s ability to detect and respond to cyber threats by simulating real-world attacks. These exercises, including red teaming, purple teaming, social engineering, vulnerability scanning, and third-party testing, reveal critical gaps in your defenses. Our tailored simulations provide actionable intelligence, helping you improve incident response, enhance security controls, and protect your brand. With our threat intelligence integration, we ensure your team is equipped to handle advanced threats, offering strategic guidance for long-term security improvements.
